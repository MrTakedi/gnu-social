Events that come with the ActivityModeration plugin
===================================================

StartDeleteNoticeForm: starting the data in the form for deleting a notice
- $DeleteForm: the delete form being shown
- $notice: notice being deleted

EndDeleteNoticeForm: ending the data in the form for deleting a notice
- $DeleteForm: the delete form being shown
- $notice: notice being deleted

StartShowDeleteForm: just before showing the delete form
- $item: the NoticeListItem object being shown

EndShowDeleteForm: just after showing the delete form
- $item: the NoticeListItem object being shown
